Return Policy

We do our best to offer our customers the best quality. If you are not completely satisfied with any purchase made on www.greenleafbookstore.com,
please contact us and return your item(s) within 7 days of delivery/ pickup for orders within Hong Kong, or within 60 days for international orders.

Refund Policy

If the product you returned is eligible for a refund, we will leave you to choose between a full refund or a credit for your next order on www.greenleafbookstore.com.
If you have any questions or concerns about our return & refund policy, please contact us at info@greenleafbookstore.com.

Donate

We welcome donations of English language books suitable for children aged 0-16 years old.
If you have more than 100 books to donate, please contact us to arrange free pick-up service.
