FAQ


What payment methods do you accept online?


We accept Visa, Master Card, Shop Pay, Paypal and AMEX.


Do you do delivery?

Yes, we do!

Hong Kong:
Free delivery for orders from 500HKD.
For orders under 500HKD, delivery fee starts at 34HKD.
Usually, orders arrive in under 4 business days.


Can we meet as MTR stations or other locations to get the books?


Sorry that we are unable to meet you in other locations such as MTR stations or at the car park etc.
We only provide the book-delivery service of "pick up at store" or "ship by courier".
Thank you for your kind understanding.


Can I place order via Whatsapp?


Customers are required to place order directly on Green Leaf Online Books Store
to ensure the accuracy of the order information and inventory management.


How do I order a book that you don't have stock/don't carry?


Please contact us to ask about specific orders. We'll be happy to help.


How long do special book orders take to arrive?


Around 4-6 weeks
